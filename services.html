Services

In Studio Photo Shoot

Liam Bourke has a state of the art studio based in Dublin, Ireland. The equipment will allow the creation of specialised photos such as passport photos, family photos and more so product photos for ecommerce websites. The custom setting allows Liam to adjust things such as light and surroundings and reduces the overall production and editing process as the settings are ideal from the get go.



Out of Studio Photo Shoot

Liam is as professional as it gets when to comes out of studio photo shoots, his work is exceptional and recognised worldwide as being some of the best. His equipment allows him to capture moments live as it’s happening. He has unique outlook on photography that he should challenge the stereotypical photos and only shoots unique items.



Video Shoot

Liam has unique talent for shooting both videos and photos professionally. Liam has made many promotional items for companies across Ireland and has specialised in the unique fitness industry. After shooting footage for two promotional videos for gyms across Dublin, these videos have benefitted the respected gyms amazingly, their memberships increased and their brand exposure has blown up as a result.
