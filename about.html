About

David has been actively working with analogue and digital cameras since he completed college in 2015 and since then developed a useful skillset and a creative eye. At DBH photography there is a sense of love for perfection and creativity which outputs eye-catching and jaw dropping work - every single time. Word of mouth over the years have created a demand from many different clients which is setting a standard of photography in Ireland. Our work goes from weddings to clothing lines and portraits to landscapes. We stride for perfection in every click and can create a fun and memorable experience for whatever your visual goal.
